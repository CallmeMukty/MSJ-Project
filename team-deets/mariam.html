The Data Scientist

Mariam, a University of Queensland doctoral scholar at the QIMR Berghofer Medical Research Institute, is redefining mental health equity through data science. As a recipient of the prestigious UZIMA-DS Pilot Project Grant, her groundbreaking research addresses neuropsychiatric disorders in African and underrepresented communities. Leveraging data science, Mariam examines the complex interplay between genetics, environment, and social determinants of health. Her innovative approach aims to bridge gaps in mental health disparities, creating impactful change for marginalized populations.
