The Health Data Scientist

Anita is a health researcher with over three years of experience in health data science and program management. Her expertise extends to digital health, where she has mentored young statisticians and software engineers, led teams in developing web-based dashboards, and utilised data analysis to inform evidence-based decision-making in health. She holds a BSc. in Epidemiology and Biostatistics and is currently reading for an MSc. in International Health and Tropical Medicine as a MasterCard Foundation scholar at the University of Oxford.

The Digital Mentor

While pursuing an undergraduate program in Kenya, which was very new at the time, Anita faced a lack of understanding from many about what she was studying, its potential, and the career options it offered. This prompted her to seek out mentors online, where she appreciated the power of sharing experiences for others to learn from. This experience motivated her to join MSJ and actively use her social media platforms which have a current collective following of ~50,000, to advocate for the integration of data science in health and provide guidance on educational opportunities.
